The Venue has four rooms and a main hall that can be booked separately or together.

Room 2

This room is a light and airy modern space that overlooks the garden. It is perfect for smaller gatherings, seating up to 21 people. This room comes complete with air conditioning and shared use of a stylish kitchen, available for a small extra charge (which even has a serving hatch to make serving refreshments ultra-easy!).
